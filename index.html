What Is Truman’s Deal, Then?

Truman only specialises in one type of medication, and it’s for ED. This makes them experts.

As we all know, us guys will only see a doctor if we’re absolutely 100% convinced we’re dying. Even if things aren’t working properly down there, most of us would still instead go check out a shady online store first. Even if the store can’t even spell ED...

“Honey, I’ve got this new thing called blah blah blah? .... Meant to be amazing.”

Truman launched only recently and quickly sought to re-imagine what is usually an embarrassing prescription process for men who have trouble with their intimate performance.

The founder of Truman wanted a safe place men could go to and get things sorted. He recognised that most men struggle to do this in person with their doctor. ED is, after all, embarrassing.

With Truman, all you have to do is sign up for your free consultation online, and answer a few quick questions about your symptoms, lifestyle and overall health.

The form will be sent to a physician. If it turns out that you’re a strong candidate for ED, you’ll be given a Truman prescription, most likely for a well-known brand that you've already heard of, and put on a treatment program.

You can fill the prescription in anywhere (even at the company’s own in-house cloud pharmacy), while a conversation with a physician will take place over the phone.
